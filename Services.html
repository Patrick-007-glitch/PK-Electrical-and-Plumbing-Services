Our Services

At PK Electrical and Plumbing Services, we provide a comprehensive range of professional services designed to keep your home, business, or property safe, efficient, and dependable. Electrical wiring and installation for new construction or construction sites.

Our Plumbing Services Include

Detection and repair of leaks to avoid water damage.

Installation, repair, or replacement of pipes, fixtures, and fittings.

To clear blockages for continued flow and for clearing out drain lines.

Water heater installation, servicing, and repair

In general, makeovers and updates for bathroom and kitchen plumbing.

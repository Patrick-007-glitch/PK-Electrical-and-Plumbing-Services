About Our Objectives and Goals

Founded this year, PK Electrical and Plumbing Services consists of passionate workers eager to deliver high-quality electronic and plumbing services to our community. Our staff is experienced and led by hands-on, technically adept people. We are committed to providing quality work you can depend on.

Every job, no matter the size, receives our full attention and commitment. Our technicians stay updated with the latest industry standards and technologies to ensure safe, efficient, and modern solutions. Our mission is to build long-term relationships with customers through honesty, expertise, and consistent results.

Our History

PK Electrical and Plumbing Services was originally a small company created in my local community. My name is Patrick Tshiluwa Kamunga, founder of PK Electrical and Plumbing Services. Created this year, we have grown rapidly to a company located in the CBD of Cape Town, competing with big corporations in the industry.

At first, I worked as a self-employed handyman. I later brought together a group of colleagues and friends with the same vision. In just 4 months, we built a team of 24 experienced electricians and plumbers. Our work gained recognition when we completed the electrical and plumbing installations for the community centre built this year.

My goal for this company is to see it grow not only regionally but nationally as well.

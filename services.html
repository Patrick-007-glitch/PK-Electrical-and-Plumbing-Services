Our Services

At PK Electrical and Plumbing Services, we provide a comprehensive range of professional services designed to keep your home, business, or property safe, efficient, and dependable. We have a skilled team that specializes in electrical and plumbing work for everything from basic repairs to large-scale installations.

Electrical Services

Electrical wiring and installation for new construction.

We provide safe and efficient electrical wiring for new homes and buildings, ensuring all circuits are installed to code and ready for use.

Retail lighting, retrofitting, and energy-saving ideas.

Upgrade your lighting system with modern, energy-efficient solutions that reduce costs and enhance the atmosphere of your store or office.

Circuit breaker repair and installation.

We install and repair circuit breakers to protect your home or business from electrical overloads and keep your systems safe.

Diagnosing and fixing electrical problems.

Our technicians quickly identify electrical faults and fix them efficiently to minimize downtime and keep your property powered safely.

Appliance and outlet installation and repair.

We install and repair outlets, switches, and appliances, ensuring safe connections and reliable operation throughout your property.

Plumbing Services

Leak detection and repair.

We locate leaks quickly and repair them efficiently, preventing water damage and saving on unnecessary water bills.

Pipe and fixture repair or replacement.

We repair or replace pipes and plumbing fixtures to ensure your plumbing system works reliably and safely.

Drain cleaning and blockage removal.

We remove clogs and clean drains efficiently, preventing backups and keeping your water flowing smoothly.

Water heater installation, servicing, and repair.

We install, maintain, and repair water heaters to ensure your home always has hot water safely and efficiently.

Bathroom and kitchen plumbing updates.

We modernize your bathrooms and kitchens, updating plumbing systems for better efficiency, safety, and aesthetics.
